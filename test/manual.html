Meeting Tools Manual v2.5.0

This application is an all-in-one suite for Toastmasters Timers and Grammarians/Ah-Counters. It works offline and saves all data locally to your device browser.

1. Word of the Day (WOD)

The Grammarian can now generate and set the Word of the Day directly within the app.

  • Generate: Tap the ★ Word of the Day card. Tap Next to cycle through 1,000+ curated sophisticated words.
  • Definitions & Usage: Definitions and example sentences are fetched automatically (requires internet during selection).
  • Selection: Tapping Select does two things:
    1. It locks the word, definition, and usage into the final Meeting Report.
    2. It automatically adds that word as a button in the Ah-Counter tracker.

2. Grammarian & Ah-Counter

Smart Count Merging

You no longer have to worry about tracking the Toastmaster or General Evaluator in multiple segments. If you track the same person in the same role twice, the app automatically merges the counts into one single entry in the report.

The Tally Interface

  • Tap Center: Increment the count (+1).
  • Bottom Right (minus): Correct a mistake (-1).
  • Bottom Left (red X): Permanently delete a word from your meeting list.
  • Add Word: Use the input at the top to add a custom crutch word on the fly.

3. Timer Functions

Select a name and a speech type. The screen will change color based on the following logic:

  • Green: Minimum time reached (e.g., 5:00 for a 5-7m speech).
  • Yellow: Target time reached (e.g., 6:00).
  • Red: Maximum time reached (e.g., 7:00).

Tap Commit & Exit to save the result. Tap Custom at the bottom of the grid to set unique thresholds for non-standard speeches.

4. Reporting & Sharing

Pro Tip: Use the Copy button if you want to paste the results into a specific Slack channel or Email. Use the Share button to send the full report directly to the Notes app.
  • Meeting Report: Shows the Word of the Day section followed by a table of all recorded times and filler counts.
  • Markdown Export: Toggle this in Settings to generate code that creates beautiful tables in apps like GitHub, Obsidian, or professional email clients.

5. Data Management

  • Roster: Names are saved permanently. Use the Search bar in Setup to quickly find a member.
  • Backup/Restore: In Settings, you can export your entire roster and filler list as a .json file. This is useful for moving your data to a new iPad or sharing the club list with another member.
  • Clear Meeting Data: This clears the session logs and the Word of the Day. Note: This does not delete your member roster.
