Meeting Timer Guide

This high-visibility tool is designed for timing speeches, evaluations, and impromptu sessions. It scales seamlessly across smartphones, tablets, and desktops.

⚠️ Data Privacy & Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. This app does not use a cloud database. Speaker names, club settings, and meeting logs are stored entirely within your device's web browser memory (Local Storage).

  • Browser Specific: Switching browsers requires a new setup or using the Export/Import tool.
  • Persistence: Your Roster and Settings will remain even if you close the tab, provided you don't clear your browser's "Website Data."

1. Getting Started

  • Club Personalization: Tap the blue subtitle on the main screen to set your club name for reports.
  • Settings & Portability: Use the Settings icon to access the Import/Export Roster feature. This allows you to move your saved member list between devices (e.g., from a laptop to a tablet).
  • Session Management: Tap Clear Meeting Logs at the start of a new meeting to reset the current session's report.

2. Setting Up a Speech

Speaker Selection

  • Automatic Formatting: Names are automatically converted to UPPERCASE for maximum visibility.
  • Smart Search: Type a name to filter your saved roster. Tap a name to select it.
  • Quick Add: Type a new name and tap Add to save them to your local roster permanently.

Speech Types & Custom Times

  • Choose from standard presets (Table Topics, Evaluation, etc.).
  • Custom Time Memory: When using Custom Time, the app now remembers your last-entered Green, Yellow, and Red thresholds so you don't have to re-type them for consecutive custom speakers.

3. Using the Timer

Tap START TIMING to begin. The clock uses an Auto-Start sequence to ensure accuracy.

  • High-Contrast Transitions: The screen transitions through White, Green, Yellow, and Red based on the selected timing.
  • Control Flow: Use PAUSE for interruptions and RESUME to continue.
  • Commit & Exit: Once a speech is finished, you must tap the COMMIT & EXIT button. This stops the background process and saves the final time to your Meeting Report.

4. Reporting & Sharing

The Meeting Report maintains a log of every "Committed" speech in the current session.

  • Sharing: Tap Share Report to copy the text to your clipboard (Desktop) or open the system share sheet (Mobile) to send results via Email or SMS.

5. Advanced Features

  • Offline Capability: Once loaded, the app requires no internet to function.
  • Home Screen App: Use your browser's "Add to Home Screen" option to use the timer as a full-screen, standalone app without browser bars.
