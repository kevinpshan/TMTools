1. Word of the Day (W.O.D.)

The W.O.D. feature uses ChatGPT-4o-mini to provide intelligent, club-appropriate content.

  • Randomize: Tap "Random" to pick from a curated library of 1,000 sophisticated words.
  • Search: Type a specific word into the search bar and tap the search button to fetch a custom AI definition and usage sentence for that word.
  • Contextual AI: ChatGPT is instructed to provide "public speaking" flavored definitions and family-friendly examples.
  • Select: Tapping "Select" pins the word to the top-left of the Ah-Counter grid with a gold "WORD OF DAY" badge for easy tracking.
The W.O.D. feature requires an OpenAI API key. See Section 5 for setup instructions.

2. Timer Functionality

  • Presets: Quickly select from standard Toastmasters timing slots — 1-2m (Table Topics), 2-3m (Evaluations), 4-6m (Ice Breaker), or 5-7m (Speeches).
  • Custom: Tap "Custom" to enter your own Green / Yellow / Red thresholds in minutes for non-standard segments.
  • Visual Cues: The entire screen background turns Green at the minimum time, Yellow at the target time, and Red at the maximum time — visible from across the room.
  • Pause: Tap "Pause" to freeze the clock without losing elapsed time. Tap "Resume" to continue from where you stopped.
  • Commit & Exit: Saves the final time to the meeting report and returns to the menu. The background color resets automatically.

3. Ah-Counter & Filler Words

  • Tracking: Tap any filler word card to increment the count. Tap the small "−" button in the bottom-right corner to decrement if you over-counted.
  • Word of the Day: If a W.O.D. has been selected, it appears in the top-left position with a gold border. Tap it each time the speaker uses the word correctly.
  • Session Merging: If you save a session and start a new one for the same speaker and role, the app automatically adds the counts together — useful for long presentations delivered in parts.
  • Delete Fillers: Tap the red "✕" on any card to remove a filler word you are not tracking this meeting. Deleted fillers can be restored by re-importing a backup via Settings.

4. Speech Transcription (Grammarian Mode)

When running an Ah-Counter session, you can enable automatic speech transcription to detect filler words hands-free using OpenAI Whisper.

How to use it

  • Enable: On the Setup screen in Ah-Counter mode, toggle on Record Speech before tapping START. This toggle only appears in Ah-Counter mode.
  • Recording: The transcription screen appears. Tap Start Recording and hold your device toward the speaker. The status indicator pulses red while active.
  • End the speech: Tap End Speech when the speaker finishes. The audio uploads to OpenAI Whisper — transcription typically takes 5 to 15 seconds depending on length.
  • Review results: The full transcript appears with filler words highlighted in red and the Word of the Day highlighted in gold. A count summary is shown below the transcript.
  • Merge to Counter: Tap Merge to Counter to load the counter screen with the detected counts pre-filled. Adjust any counts manually before tapping Save Results.

Browser & device compatibility

Recording has been tested and confirmed working on iPad with Chrome. If you experience microphone access issues on other browsers or devices, try Safari as an alternative. Behavior may vary on older iOS versions or in installed PWA mode on iPhone.

Tips for best results

  • Hold the device 1 to 2 feet from the speaker for the clearest audio pickup.
  • Whisper performs best in a reasonably quiet room — heavy background noise reduces accuracy.
  • If audience members speak during the session, you can manually correct any miscounted fillers on the counter screen after merging.
  • The Record Speech toggle resets to off each time you return to the Setup screen, so it will never activate accidentally.
Requires an OpenAI API key. Transcription uses the whisper-1 model. Cost is very low — a typical 5 to 7 minute speech costs roughly $0.01 to $0.05. See Section 5 for key setup.

5. Setting up AI Usage (ChatGPT)

Why do I need a key? For privacy and cost reasons, the app does not store a central key. By providing your own, your meeting data stays private and you control your own usage and billing.

How to get your OpenAI API Key

Step 1: Go to platform.openai.com and sign up or log in.
Step 2: Navigate to the API Keys tab in the left sidebar.
Step 3: Click + Create new secret key. Name it "Toastmasters App" and copy the key — it starts with sk-....
Step 4: In the Meeting Tools app, go to Settings (⚙) and paste the key into the ChatGPT API Key field.

OpenAI provides a small amount of free credit to new accounts. After that, both gpt-4o-mini (Word of the Day) and whisper-1 (transcription) are very inexpensive — typical meeting usage costs a few cents per month at most.

6. Data Management

  • Markdown Export: Toggle this on in Settings to format the meeting report as a Markdown table — useful for pasting into Slack, email, or Discord.
  • Backup Data: Downloads a JSON file containing your complete speaker roster, filler word list, club name, and API key. Save this somewhere safe as your recovery option.
  • Restore Data: Imports a previously saved JSON backup. This will overwrite your current roster and settings.
  • Clear Meeting Data: Resets all session logs and the selected W.O.D. for the next meeting. This does not delete your speaker roster or settings.
